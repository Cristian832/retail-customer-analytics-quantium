This project analyzes supermarket transaction and customer data to understand who drives chip sales and to evaluate whether a retail trial delivered a real commercial impact. 
The analysis combines customer segmentation insights with a trial vs control store methodology to support strategic category decisions.

Business Problem
The Category Manager for Chips required:
- A clear understanding of customer purchasing behavior within the Chips category.
- Identification of high-value customer segments and key sales drivers.
- Validation of a retail trial to determine whether it delivered measurable sales uplift beyond normal store variation.
